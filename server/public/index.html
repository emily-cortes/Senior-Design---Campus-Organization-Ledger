Senior Design Project

Campus Organization Ledger

A finance dashboard for student organizations to review balances, record transactions, and keep treasurers and advisors on the same page.

Profile customization and additional personalization are planned for a future update.

Prototype Notice

This is a prototype.

Feedback is greatly appreciated, and designs or features may change as the senior design project continues to develop.

Purpose

Built to replace scattered spreadsheets and unclear fund tracking.

The goal is to give campus organizations one place to see current balances, spending history, and pending requests with a cleaner workflow.

Current Focus

  • Organization-level fund tracking
  • Transaction entry and visibility
  • Support for treasurer and advisor roles
  • A foundation for future approvals and reporting
